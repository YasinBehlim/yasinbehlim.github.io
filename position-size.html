Quick Guide to Position Sizing

Risk only 1–2% of your account per trade to survive losing streaks.

Formula: Position Size (USD) = (Balance × Risk %) / |Entry - Stop| %

Then: Coins to buy = Position USD / Entry Price

Example: $10,000 account, risk 1% ($100), entry $50,000, stop $48,000 → risk distance 4% → position ~$2,500 USD (~0.05 BTC).

Use this before opening trades to avoid over-risking. Adjust for leverage if using futures.
