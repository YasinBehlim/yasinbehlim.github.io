How Profit & Loss Works (Quick Guide)

Spot trading (no leverage): PnL = (Sell - Buy) × Quantity - Fees

Futures / Leveraged trades: PnL = (Sell - Buy) × Quantity × Leverage - Fees

Return %: (PnL / (Buy Price × Quantity)) × 100%

Always include exchange fees, funding rates (for perps), and potential slippage in real trades.
