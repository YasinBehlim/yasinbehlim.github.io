Crypto Trading Basics – Quick Understanding

Short explanations of essential concepts for beginners. Apply them directly with the free calculators above.

1. Risk Management & Position Sizing

Limit risk to 1–2% of your account per trade to survive losing streaks. Position size depends on account balance, risk %, and stop-loss distance.

Try Position Size Calculator →

2. Profit & Loss (PnL) Calculation

For longs: PnL = (Exit - Entry) × Quantity × Leverage multiplier. Always factor in fees. Know realized vs unrealized PnL before closing.

Test with Profit/Loss Calculator →

3. Leverage & Liquidation

Leverage multiplies gains and losses (10x = 1% move affects 10% of margin). Liquidation happens when losses eat your margin — calculate it before trading.

Preview risks with the tools here.

4. Stablecoins & Conversions

USDT/USDC help lock in value during volatility. Understand exact rates, fees, and spreads when converting to/from fiat or other coins.

Practice with USD Converter →

5. Market vs Limit Orders

Market orders fill instantly at current price (convenient but can slip). Limit orders wait for your chosen price (more control, may not execute).
